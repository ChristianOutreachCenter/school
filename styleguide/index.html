Typeface:

Primary Typeface: Centabel

The Centabel font is free to use and we have adopted it as the primary font for COS. Other font's may be used where needed.




Download the Cantabel font here: centabel.ttf



Secondary Typeface: Fire Sans Condensed




Download the Fira Sans font here: FiraSansCondensed-Regular.ttf

More variations of the Fira Sans font can be found and downloaded here: Google Fonts


How to install the fonts (windows):

Download .ttf file, then right click on the file and click "install".
